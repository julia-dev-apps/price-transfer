Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the "Price Transfer" Google Sheets add-on uses and protects the information you provide.

Information Collection and Use

The "Price Transfer" add-on only accesses your Google Sheets data as necessary to perform its functions. It does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

External API Requests

The add-on may send requests to external APIs solely to process data required for its features. No personal or sensitive data is transmitted.

Data Security

Your data remains within your Google Sheets and is not stored or shared with any third party. We prioritize your privacy and data security.

Permissions

The add-on requests the minimum necessary permissions to function correctly, including access to your spreadsheets and the ability to run in the background.
